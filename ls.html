Learning Styles

Learning Style

There are 4 main Learning styles

Visual Auditory Read/Write Kinesthetic
Learning by seeing Learning by hearing Learning by reading or writing Learning by doing

Each learning style has its own strengths and weaknesses, and individuals may find that they benefit from a combination of these styles. I find that I personally benefit from a mix of writing and kinesthetic learning. I tend to benefit more from doing something on my own and writing it down in order to retain what I just learned.

Time Management

Time Management

Time management is the process of planning and controlling how much time to spend on specific activities. Good time management enables an individual to complete more in a shorter period of time, lowers stress, and leads to career success. Some effective time management techniques include:

  • Setting clear goals and priorities
  • Creating a schedule or to-do list
  • Avoiding multitasking
  • Taking breaks to recharge
  • Eliminating distractions
